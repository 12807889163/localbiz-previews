How fast can I get my site?
Most sites are delivered within 24 hours of receiving your business info. Sometimes same day if we get your details in the morning.
What do I need to provide?
Just your business name, phone number, email, services you offer, and the city/area you serve. Optional: a logo, photos, or specific content you want included.
Are there any ongoing fees?
No. $299 is a one-time payment. Hosting is included for the first year. After that, you can continue hosting with us at a low annual rate or take the site elsewhere if you prefer.
What if I need changes after it's built?
We include one round of revisions after delivery. If you need significant changes later, we can quote that separately — but most clients are happy with the first version.
Can I use my own domain name?
Yes. If you have a domain (e.g., yourbusiness.com), we can connect it. Domain registration is separate (~£12/year) and we can help you set that up too.
How does payment work?
Secure checkout via Stripe. You can pay with any card. We'll send you a checkout link after you reach out — just reply to our email or message us with your business name.
