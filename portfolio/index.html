Questions?

Frequently Asked

What does "24 hours" actually mean?
Once you send us your business info (name, phone, services, city), we deliver a live, working site within 24 hours. Usually faster — often same day.
Do I own the website?
You own the content and design. The site is hosted on our infrastructure for the first year included in the $299. After that, hosting is available separately at a low annual rate.
What if I want changes after it's built?
We include one round of revisions. If you need major design changes after delivery, we can discuss adjustments — but most clients are happy with the first version.
Do you offer monthly plans?
Not for the initial build. This is a one-time $299 payment. If you want ongoing hosting + maintenance after year one, we'll discuss that separately.
Can you build multi-page sites?
This offer is for a single-page site focused on conversion. If you need more pages (services, about, gallery), we can quote that separately. But for most local service businesses, one page is all you need.
How does payment work?
We use Stripe — you'll receive a secure checkout link after we confirm your order. We don't collect payment info directly. All major cards accepted.
