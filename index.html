FAQ

Common questions

How fast can I get my site?
Most sites are delivered within 24 hours of receiving your business details. If you send details in the morning, you could have a live site by evening.
What information do I need to provide?
Just your business name, phone number, email address, the services you offer, and the city or area you serve. Optional: a logo, photos, or any specific content you'd like included. That's it.
Is this a one-time payment or a subscription?
This is a one-time payment of $299. There are no monthly fees, no hosting fees, and no recurring charges from us. Your only ongoing cost would be domain registration (~£12/year) if you want to use your own domain.
What if I need changes after the site is built?
One round of revisions is included at no extra cost. If you need significant changes after that, we can discuss a small additional fee — but most clients are happy with the first version.
Can I use my own domain name?
Absolutely. If you already own a domain (like yourbusiness.com), we can connect it. We also include a free subdomain on our GitHub Pages setup while you're getting set up. We walk you through the entire domain connection process.
How does payment work?
Send us an email with your business details and we'll reply with a secure Stripe checkout link within minutes. You can pay with any credit or debit card — it's quick and fully secure.
Do you offer support after the site is live?
Yes. If anything goes wrong with your site or you have questions after delivery, just email us and we'll sort it out. We're here.
